Nifty Offset

Blurring art, ethics, and fundraising for causes we care about.

NFTs have promise and problems. Carbon emissions and increased inequity chief among them. Nifty Offset seeks to bring attention to the problems as well as test solutions for mitigation. Along the way we’ll partner with artists and causes we find meaningful, interesting or important.

DevWell

Coding education should be free. Free study resources abound but finding a mentor typically requires privileged circumstances and the curricula teaching open source technologies is itself treated as IP. DevWell looks at how to facilitate the growth of a true open source curriculum and an AI powered mentor/mentee connection bot.
